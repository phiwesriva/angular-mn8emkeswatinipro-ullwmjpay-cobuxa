ESWATINI HOUSING BOARD OPEN TO DELAYED RENTAL PAYMENT FOR MAY & JUNE

The Eswatini Housing Board, a Public Enterprise responsible for providing affordable housing for low and middle income earning EmaSwati says it is open to a leeway for late payment of May and June 2020 rentals due to COVID-19.

This is contained in a statement issued by the Public Enterprise's Managing Director, Mduduzi Dlamini. It further states that the late payments will not attract a penalty. "Tenants wishing to take advantage of this flexibility must contact the Leasing Office to make arrangements for settlement of the arrears within the 2020/2021 financial year" reads the statement.

Eswatini Housing Board owns the iconic Mobeni flats, which are situated in Mbabane and Matsapha among other properties in the country.
